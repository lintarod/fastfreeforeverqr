Tips for Event QR Codes

Test your check-in flow: Before the event, practice scanning with your staff to ensure everything works smoothly.

Have a backup: Always have a manual check-in option in case of technical issues.

Size appropriately: For badges, 0.75" minimum. For signage, scale up so it's scannable from the expected distance.

Consider lighting: QR codes can be hard to scan in very dark venues. Plan accordingly.
