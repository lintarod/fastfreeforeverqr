Types of Google Maps Links

  • Place link: Opens a specific location or business
  • Directions link: Opens navigation from user's location
  • Pin drop: Opens a specific coordinate
  • Plus code: Opens a location using Google's Plus Codes

Works with Other Map Apps

While we call this "Google Maps QR Code," you can actually use links from:

  • Google Maps (maps.google.com)
  • Apple Maps (maps.apple.com)
  • Waze (waze.com/ul)
  • Any map service with shareable links

When scanned, the phone will typically offer to open in the user's default maps app.
