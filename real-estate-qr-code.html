Best Practices for Real Estate QR Codes

Size for yard signs: At least 4x4 inches so it's scannable from the street or sidewalk. Bigger is better for drive-by traffic.

Weatherproofing: Use vinyl or laminated materials for outdoor signs. Faded or damaged QR codes won't scan.

Add a call-to-action: "Scan for Virtual Tour" or "Scan for More Photos" tells people what they'll get.

Track your scans: Use a URL shortener with analytics (like Bitly) as your destination to see how many people scan.

Test at distance: After printing, test scanning your QR code from 6+ feet away to simulate real-world use.
