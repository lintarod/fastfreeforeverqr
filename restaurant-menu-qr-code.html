Best Practices for Restaurant QR Codes

Size matters: Print your QR code at least 2x2 inches (5x5 cm) for easy scanning. For window displays, go larger.

Test before printing: Always scan your QR code with multiple phones before printing in bulk.

Add a call-to-action: Include text like "Scan for Menu" near your QR code so customers know what to do.

Use a mobile-friendly menu: Make sure your online menu works well on smartphones since that's how customers will view it.
