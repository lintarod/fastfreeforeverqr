Tips for Wedding QR Codes

Make them beautiful: Download the SVG version and place it in your invitation design. It scales perfectly at any size.

Test with older relatives: Not everyone is familiar with QR codes. Include brief instructions like "Point your phone camera here."

Have a backup: Always include a short URL or traditional RSVP option for guests who struggle with technology.

Use the same QR code everywhere: If your wedding website has all the info, you only need one QR code for everything.
