What YouTube URLs Work?

  • Video URLs: youtube.com/watch?v=XXXXXX
  • Short URLs: youtu.be/XXXXXX
  • Channel URLs: youtube.com/@channelname
  • Playlist URLs: youtube.com/playlist?list=XXXXXX
  • Timestamps: youtube.com/watch?v=XXXXXX&t=120

Best Practices for YouTube QR Codes

  • Use short URLs when possible ‚Äî youtu.be links create simpler, more scannable QR codes
  • Test on mobile ‚Äî Ensure the video plays well on phones since that's how people will watch
  • Add a call-to-action ‚Äî "Scan to Watch" or "See the Video"
  • Consider timestamps ‚Äî Link to specific moments in longer videos
  • Keep videos available ‚Äî Don't delete or private videos that QR codes link to
